What is Lumpsum Investment?

A lumpsum investment is a one-time bulk investment in a mutual fund scheme. Instead of staggering your investments across multiple months (like a SIP), you deploy the entire available capital immediately.

Lumpsum Formula

The calculation is based on the compound interest formula: FV = PV × (1 + r)^n where PV is the principal, r is the annual rate, and n is the years.
