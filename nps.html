National Pension System (NPS)

The smartest way to build your retirement wealth while enjoying exclusive tax benefits.

Tier 1 Account

The mandatory retirement account with tax benefits.

  • Lock-in until age 60
  • Min. annual contribution: ₹1,000
  • Extra ₹50,000 tax deduction u/s 80CCD(1B)
  • 60% corpus is tax-free on withdrawal, 40% mandatory annuity

Tier 2 Account

A voluntary investment account with greater flexibility.

  • No lock-in period (withdraw anytime)
  • No minimum annual contribution after initial setup
  • No tax deductions available
  • Requires an active Tier 1 account
