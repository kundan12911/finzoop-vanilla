Frequently Asked Questions

Does checking loan offers map my CIBIL score?

No, checking eligibility on Finzoop generates a "soft pull" which does not impact your credit score at all.

What is the minimum CIBIL score required for a Personal Loan?

Ideally, a score above 750 gets you the best interest rates. However, some NBFCs provide loans for scores as low as 650 with a marginally higher interest rate.

Are there any prepayment penalties?

For floating rate home loans, banks cannot charge prepayment penalties as per RBI guidelines. For personal and business loans, it varies from 1% to 4% depending on the lender.
